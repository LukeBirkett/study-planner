if you are looking for a follow-up paper that pushed the spatial tracking even further, look into their 2020 group publication:  Herrera-Diestra, J. L., et al. (2020). Ball Flow Networks (BFN) framework.This tracking network research explicitly details the Pitch-Passing and Ball Flow paradigms using high-resolution positional tracking data provided by FC Barcelona to isolate tactical advancement through physical pitch corridors without getting distracted by player identity.
